About Us

RAZONES, based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, was established to provide high-quality CAD-CAM, pattern and die design and manufacturing, new product development, laser cutting and industrial consulting services to customers in the foundry and machining industries. Our team of experienced engineers and technicians use state-of-the-art technology to create precise and accurate patterns, molds, and dies, ensuring that the final product meets the highest quality standards. We understand that every customer has unique needs and requirements, which is why we work closely with each client to understand their specific needs and provide customized solutions. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help your business.

CAD-CAM

Pattern and die design and manufacturing

RAZONES, specializes in the design and manufacturing of patterns and dies for the foundry industry. Using state-of-the-art technology, our team of experienced engineers and technicians create precise and accurate patterns that can withstand the rigors of the casting process. We also offer custom die solutions for specific applications, such as investment casting and sand casting.

New Product Development for Machining shops

RAZONES, offers comprehensive new product development services for machining shops. Our team of experts, with expertise in CAD-CAM, 3D modeling and prototyping, work closely with clients to understand their specific needs and provide custom solutions. From concept development to final production, we ensure that our clients are satisfied with the end product.

Laser cutting

RAZONES, provides laser cutting services for a sheet metals. Our state-of-the-art laser cutting equipment can handle both simple and complex shapes, and we use advanced CAD-CAM software to ensure that the final product meets the highest quality standards.

Industrial consulting

RAZONES, offers industrial consulting services to help customers improve their production processes and increase efficiency. Our team of experts, with extensive experience in the foundry and machining industries, can provide guidance on a wide range of topics such as process optimization, quality control, and cost reduction
